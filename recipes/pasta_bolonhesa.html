Pasta Bolognese

Description

Pasta Bolognese is a classic Italian dish that is actually very easy to make at home! With a handful of ingredients and 30 minutes of time, you'll have a complex sauce that's perfect over pasta and can even be used in other Italian dishes, such as lasagne.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 125g pancetta finely diced
  • 500g ground beef I use 90% lean
  • 3/4 cup onion finely diced
  • 1/2 cup carrot peeled and finely diced
  • 2 teaspoons garlic minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 250g can tomato sauce
  • 800g can crushed tomatoes do not drain
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 350g spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese grated
  • 1/4 cup basil leaves thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the pancetta and cook for 4-5 minutes until browned and crispy.
  2. Add the ground beef to the pan and season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for 5 minutes, breaking up the beef into small pieces with a spatula.
  3. Add the onion and carrot to the pan. Cook for 4-5 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Add the garlic and Italian seasoning, then cook for 30 seconds. Drain off any excess grease.
  4. Add the red wine to the pot and bring to a simmer. Cook for 3-4 minutes or until wine is reduced by half.
  5. Stir in the tomato sauce and crushed tomatoes. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Bring the sauce to a simmer. Cook for 25-30 minutes or until sauce has thickened.
  7. Stir in the heavy cream and cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Cook the spaghetti in salted water according to package directions. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta cooking water.
  9. Pour the pasta in with the sauce and toss to combine. If the sauce is too thick, add a little pasta cooking water to thin it out.
  10. Top the pasta with basil and parmesan cheese, then serve immediately.
